The Beauty Of Nature

Mother Nature is the sweetest of all blessings of God. Her bounty is inexhaustible and her charms are manifold. She never grows tired of bestowing her favors on us and showers us with her riches in all season.

Beauty is the pleasant and appealing sensation to our eyes. It is enhanced by symmetry, color, shape, smell, scent, dynamism and vibration, brightness and darkness and other factors.
